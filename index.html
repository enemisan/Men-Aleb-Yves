About Us

The heart of Yves Douaihy stopped beating as a result of a Sudden Cardiac Arrest.

To honor his memory and help assist when such tragedies happen again, MenalebYves was founded on May 31, 2021, which also marked Yves' birthday.

JOIN US

Our mission is to provide public spaces and sports arenas with First Aid Medical Supplies
and the Automated External Defibrillator.

We work on raising awareness among the youth and promoting humanitarian efforts.

Medical

To make life-saving medical equipment and first aid materials accessible in as many public places as possible.

Humanitarian

To provide humanitarian efforts to our community, such as healthcare, education, personal support, and economic aid.

Awareness

To raise awareness and spread knowledge about how to respond effectively in emergency situations.
